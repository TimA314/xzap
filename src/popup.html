Embed Your Address in an Image

Add a QR code to an image that can be scanned to retrieve your Lightning Address. To enable zaps on X, you must add the QR code to your main profile picture (not the banner) on the X platform. Users with the XZap extension will see a Zap button on your X posts if your profile picture contains the QR code.
